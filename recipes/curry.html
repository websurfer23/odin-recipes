Creamy Chickpea Coconut Curry

Description

This one-pot curry is rich, comforting, and packed with plant-based protein. Chickpeas simmer in a fragrant coconut milk sauce infused with garlic, ginger, and warm spices. Itâ€™s a dish that feels both nourishing and luxurious, especially when served over fluffy rice or with warm naan.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil (15 ml)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped (approx. 150 g)
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated fresh ginger (15 g)
  • 1 tbsp curry powder (15 g)
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 400 g canned chickpeas, drained
  • 400 ml canned coconut milk
  • 150 g chopped tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  • 100 g spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked basmati rice or naan, to serve

Steps

  1. heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Saute onion for 5 minutes until soft.
  3. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute
  4. Stir in spices, the add chickpeas, coconut milk, and tomatoes.
  5. Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add spinach, cook until wilted (2-3minutes)
  7. Season to taste and serve hot with rice or naan.
