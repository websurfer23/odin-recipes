Honey-Lime Sweet Potato, Black Bean & Corn Tacos

Description

These vibrant tacos are a celebration of colour and texture. Roasted sweet potatoes bring natural sweetness, black beans add protein and creaminess, and corn delivers crunch—all wrapped in warm tortillas and topped with a zesty honey-lime dressing. It’s a meatless meal that feels indulgent, with just the right balance of sweet, tangy, and savoury.
