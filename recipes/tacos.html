Honey-Lime Sweet Potato, Black Bean & Corn Tacos

Description

These vibrant tacos are a celebration of colour and texture. Roasted sweet potatoes bring natural sweetness, black beans add protein and creaminess, and corn delivers crunch—all wrapped in warm tortillas and topped with a zesty honey-lime dressing. It’s a meatless meal that feels indulgent, with just the right balance of sweet, tangy, and savoury.

Ingredients

  • 400 g sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (15ml)
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 240 g canned black beans, drained
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 tbsp honey (15 ml)
  • Juice of 1 lime (approx. 30ml)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: avocado, coriander, pickled onion, sour cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Toss sweet potatoes with olive oil, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roast for 25 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and tender.
  4. Warm tortillas in a dry pan or oven.
  5. Mix honey and lime juice in a small bowl to make the dressing.
  6. Assemble tacos with sweet potato, black beans, corn, and toppings.
  7. Drizzle dressing over each taco and serve immediately
